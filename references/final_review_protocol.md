# Final Review Protocol

Stage 9 performs the final audit after paper generation.

## Required Outputs

- `workspace/output/final/review_report.md`
- `workspace/output/final/anonymity_report.md`
- `workspace/output/final/quality_report.md`

## Review Coverage

Check at least:
- problem coverage across all questions
- conflicts between `problem.md` and `reference.pdf`
- verification completion
- sensitivity completion
- figure and table citation consistency
- notation and unit consistency
- hard-number source integrity
- anonymity risks
- PDF generation status

## Report Roles

### `review_report.md`

Summarize overall paper readiness, major risks, and any required follow-up before the document is considered complete.

### `anonymity_report.md`

Check author, team, school, path, metadata, screenshot, and narrative leakage risks.

### `quality_report.md`

Review coherence, evidence discipline, figure and table quality, writing consistency, and whether the paper stays inside the locked evidence boundary.

## Exclusion

- Do not introduce a submission-packaging stage here.
- Final review ends with reviewed artifacts, not with a generated competition package.
